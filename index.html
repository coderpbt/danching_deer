What we’re about

Your Dancing Deer NFT avatar is your key into the Dancing Deer Disco. There are 9,999 unique Deer avatars that have been randomly generated on the Ethereum blockchain for you to collect. Some Deer are rarer than others. Deer can have up to 11 traits out of a possible 200+.

Once you buy your Deer, you own all IP and possess all commercial rights for the image and artwork as long as you hold the Deer in your wallet. Rights are transferred to the new owner anytime a Deer is sold on secondary. There is a 5% royalty for all secondary transactions that will be used to fund the new Deer NFT collection.

The Dancing Deer Disco Story

Welcome to our fine collection of dancing deer NFT’s It all started when on a Saturday night we dragged our mascot Deer into our nightclub and put it in the middle of the dancefloor, at first people were a little thrown off by its appearance but after a few minutes/drinks they accepted its presence and started putting hats and glasses on to it’s head and putting scarfs around his neck and all other kinds of stuff to make the deer look more like every person in the club. At that time we started thinking about an alternative reality where Deer would live like us humans and also go out just like us! Imagine a street full of Deer, clubs filled with Dancing Deer, every deer in his/her own style, trying to impress

the ladies/gents , offices with Deer sitting at a desk, in a suit, making phone calls, deer driving buses, deer making sandwiches, deer mowing the lawn, what a crazy world would that be, right? This was the birth of the ‘’Dancing Deer’’ and with these NFT’s we try to get this ‘’child of our imagination’’ a little closer to reality and for everyone to go along in this crazy fantasy story. Also by making & minting this NFT collection we are trying to combine our love for disco & partying with our love for wildlife & nature. That’s why we are donating a percentage of our raised funds from this NFT collection to the WWF and Mule Deer Foundation.
